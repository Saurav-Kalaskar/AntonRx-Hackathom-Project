Aetna Immunology Medical Policy - Secukinumab (Cosentyx)
Effective Date: July 2025 | Policy Number: AET-IMM-2025-COSENTYX

================================================================================
SECTION 1: Covered Indications
================================================================================

- Moderate-to-severe plaque psoriasis in adults requiring systemic therapy (ICD-10: L40.0)
- Active psoriatic arthritis with inadequate response to conventional therapy (ICD-10: L40.50)
- Active ankylosing spondylitis and non-radiographic axial spondyloarthritis (ICD-10: M45.9)

================================================================================
SECTION 2: Prior Authorization Requirements
================================================================================

Requests must include specialist diagnosis, affected body surface area or disease activity score, prior therapy history, and screening for tuberculosis.

================================================================================
SECTION 3: Step Therapy Requirements
================================================================================

Step therapy requires trial of methotrexate or other systemic non-biologic treatment unless contraindicated due to toxicity risk or comorbid conditions.

================================================================================
SECTION 4: Site of Care Restrictions
================================================================================

Subcutaneous biologic administration is covered in outpatient settings with allowance for home administration after patient training.

================================================================================
SECTION 5: Dosing and Administration
================================================================================

Typical regimen is 300 mg subcutaneous weekly for five weeks followed by 300 mg every four weeks for maintenance.
