Cigna Immunology Medical Policy - Secukinumab (Cosentyx)
Effective Date: July 2025 | Policy Number: CIG-IMM-2025-COSENTYX

================================================================================
SECTION 1: Covered Indications
================================================================================

- Plaque psoriasis requiring biologic intervention after inadequate response to topical and phototherapy pathways (ICD-10: L40.0)
- Psoriatic arthritis with persistent active synovitis (ICD-10: L40.50)
- Ankylosing spondylitis with objective inflammation on imaging or labs (ICD-10: M45.9)

================================================================================
SECTION 2: Prior Authorization Requirements
================================================================================

Coverage requires specialist notes, severity metrics, prior systemic treatment record, and latent TB evaluation prior to biologic initiation.

================================================================================
SECTION 3: Step Therapy Requirements
================================================================================

Coverage may be non-preferred when no trial of formulary biologic alternatives or documented contraindication exists.

================================================================================
SECTION 4: Site of Care Restrictions
================================================================================

Medication may be initiated in clinic and maintained through supervised home self-administration with routine follow-up.

================================================================================
SECTION 5: Dosing and Administration
================================================================================

Standard maintenance schedule is 300 mg every four weeks following loading doses per labeled protocol.
