Cigna Neurology Medical Policy - Ocrelizumab (Ocrevus)
Effective Date: June 2025 | Policy Number: CIG-NEUR-2025-OCREVUS

================================================================================
SECTION 1: Covered Indications
================================================================================

- Relapsing forms of multiple sclerosis with clinical or MRI activity (ICD-10: G35)
- Primary progressive multiple sclerosis in adults with documented neurological decline (ICD-10: G35)

================================================================================
SECTION 2: Prior Authorization Requirements
================================================================================

Prior authorization requires neurology consultation, MRI reports, baseline immunoglobulin levels, hepatitis screening, and prior disease-modifying therapy history.

================================================================================
SECTION 3: Step Therapy Requirements
================================================================================

Step therapy requires trial of one preferred disease-modifying therapy unless rapidly evolving severe disease is documented by MRI and clinical relapse burden.

================================================================================
SECTION 4: Site of Care Restrictions
================================================================================

Infusion must occur in accredited outpatient infusion center with monitoring capabilities for infusion reactions.

================================================================================
SECTION 5: Dosing and Administration
================================================================================

Initial course includes two 300 mg infusions separated by two weeks followed by 600 mg intravenous infusion every six months.
