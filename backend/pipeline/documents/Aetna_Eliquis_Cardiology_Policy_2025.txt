Aetna Cardiology Medical Policy - Apixaban (Eliquis)
Effective Date: April 2025 | Policy Number: AET-CARD-2025-ELIQUIS

================================================================================
SECTION 1: Covered Indications
================================================================================

- Nonvalvular Atrial Fibrillation stroke prevention (ICD-10: I48.91)
- Treatment of deep vein thrombosis and pulmonary embolism (ICD-10: I82.409, I26.99)
- Reduction of recurrent DVT and PE risk after completion of initial therapy

================================================================================
SECTION 2: Prior Authorization Requirements
================================================================================

Requests must include diagnosis confirmation, renal and hepatic function, body weight, and baseline bleeding risk review. Medication reconciliation is required for anticoagulant interactions.

================================================================================
SECTION 3: Step Therapy Requirements
================================================================================

Step therapy requires trial of at least one formulary anticoagulant alternative when clinically safe. Exceptions apply for prior major bleeding on alternative regimens.

================================================================================
SECTION 4: Site of Care Restrictions
================================================================================

Oral anticoagulation is authorized for outpatient management without infusion center requirement.

================================================================================
SECTION 5: Dosing and Administration
================================================================================

Typical dose is 5 mg orally twice daily, with 2.5 mg twice daily dose-reduction criteria based on age, weight, and creatinine thresholds.
