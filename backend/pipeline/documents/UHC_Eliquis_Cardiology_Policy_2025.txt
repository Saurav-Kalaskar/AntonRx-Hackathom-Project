UnitedHealthcare Cardiology Medical Policy - Apixaban (Eliquis)
Effective Date: April 2025 | Policy Number: UHC-CARD-2025-ELIQUIS

================================================================================
SECTION 1: Covered Indications
================================================================================

- Nonvalvular atrial fibrillation stroke prevention (ICD-10: I48.91)
- DVT and PE treatment with continuation into secondary prevention (ICD-10: I82.409, I26.99)
- Peri-discharge thrombosis prevention in high-risk medically ill adults when clinically justified

================================================================================
SECTION 2: Prior Authorization Requirements
================================================================================

Coverage requires diagnosis confirmation, medication history, baseline renal function, and bleeding risk stratification. Requests must include any history of intracranial hemorrhage.

================================================================================
SECTION 3: Step Therapy Requirements
================================================================================

Step therapy is required unless patient has instability on warfarin despite monitoring or documented adverse effects with other preferred anticoagulants.

================================================================================
SECTION 4: Site of Care Restrictions
================================================================================

Therapy is covered for outpatient dispensing and routine ambulatory follow-up without infusion setting restrictions.

================================================================================
SECTION 5: Dosing and Administration
================================================================================

Recommended dose is 5 mg orally twice daily and can be reduced to 2.5 mg twice daily when dosing criteria are met.
