UnitedHealthcare Nephrology Medical Policy - Dapagliflozin (Farxiga)
Effective Date: August 2025 | Policy Number: UHC-NEPH-2025-FARXIGA

================================================================================
SECTION 1: Covered Indications
================================================================================

- Chronic kidney disease progression risk reduction in adults with albuminuric kidney disease (ICD-10: N18.9)
- Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus with chronic kidney disease and elevated renal risk profile (ICD-10: E11.22)
- Heart failure with reduced ejection fraction with renal-protective treatment objectives (ICD-10: I50.22)

================================================================================
SECTION 2: Prior Authorization Requirements
================================================================================

Requests must include CKD staging, baseline eGFR, urine albumin-creatinine ratio, and current renin-angiotensin system therapy plan.

================================================================================
SECTION 3: Step Therapy Requirements
================================================================================

Step therapy requires documented use of ACE inhibitor or ARB unless contraindicated, plus clinical rationale for SGLT2 inhibitor initiation.

================================================================================
SECTION 4: Site of Care Restrictions
================================================================================

Therapy is managed as outpatient oral treatment with periodic laboratory monitoring and nephrology follow-up as indicated.

================================================================================
SECTION 5: Dosing and Administration
================================================================================

Dose is 10 mg orally once daily with continuation based on renal trajectory and overall clinical benefit.
