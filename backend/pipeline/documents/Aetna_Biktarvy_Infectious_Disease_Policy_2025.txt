Aetna Infectious Disease Medical Policy - Bictegravir Emtricitabine Tenofovir (Biktarvy)
Effective Date: August 2025 | Policy Number: AET-INF-2025-BIKTARVY

================================================================================
SECTION 1: Covered Indications
================================================================================

- Human immunodeficiency virus type 1 treatment in treatment-naive adults and adolescents (ICD-10: B20)
- Regimen switch in virologically suppressed adults without resistance to regimen components (ICD-10: B20)

================================================================================
SECTION 2: Prior Authorization Requirements
================================================================================

Coverage requires HIV diagnosis confirmation, baseline viral load, CD4 count, resistance history review, and hepatitis B status.

================================================================================
SECTION 3: Step Therapy Requirements
================================================================================

Step therapy requires use of guideline-aligned first-line antiretroviral options unless resistance profile or adherence considerations justify direct use.

================================================================================
SECTION 4: Site of Care Restrictions
================================================================================

Oral antiretroviral therapy is covered through outpatient specialty pharmacy with adherence support program enrollment.

================================================================================
SECTION 5: Dosing and Administration
================================================================================

Dose is one tablet orally once daily with renal and hepatic monitoring based on clinical status.
