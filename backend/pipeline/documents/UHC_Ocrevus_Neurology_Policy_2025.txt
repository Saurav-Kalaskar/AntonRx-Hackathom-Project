UnitedHealthcare Neurology Medical Policy - Ocrelizumab (Ocrevus)
Effective Date: June 2025 | Policy Number: UHC-NEUR-2025-OCREVUS

================================================================================
SECTION 1: Covered Indications
================================================================================

- Relapsing-remitting multiple sclerosis with objective clinical or radiographic progression (ICD-10: G35)
- Primary progressive multiple sclerosis with worsening disability trajectory (ICD-10: G35)

================================================================================
SECTION 2: Prior Authorization Requirements
================================================================================

Coverage requires specialist documentation, MRI evidence, baseline CBC, liver panel, hepatitis B screening, and immunoglobulin status review.

================================================================================
SECTION 3: Step Therapy Requirements
================================================================================

Coverage is non-preferred when lower-cost disease-modifying agents have not been attempted and no high-activity disease rationale is provided.

================================================================================
SECTION 4: Site of Care Restrictions
================================================================================

Infusions are authorized in ambulatory infusion centers and hospital outpatient departments with emergency support availability.

================================================================================
SECTION 5: Dosing and Administration
================================================================================

Loading consists of 300 mg intravenous infusions two weeks apart, followed by maintenance infusion of 600 mg every six months.
