Cigna Cardiology Medical Policy - Apixaban (Eliquis)
Effective Date: April 2025 | Policy Number: CIG-CARD-2025-ELIQUIS

================================================================================
SECTION 1: Covered Indications
================================================================================

- Atrial fibrillation stroke and systemic embolism prevention (ICD-10: I48.91)
- Venous thromboembolism treatment and extended secondary prevention (ICD-10: I82.409)
- Post-hospitalization VTE prevention in selected high-risk patients

================================================================================
SECTION 2: Prior Authorization Requirements
================================================================================

Prior authorization requires diagnosis notes, baseline CBC, renal function tests, and assessment of concomitant antiplatelet use. Prescriber must document bleeding risk counseling.

================================================================================
SECTION 3: Step Therapy Requirements
================================================================================

Coverage may be non-preferred when no prior trial of one formulary direct oral anticoagulant is documented and no contraindication is provided.

================================================================================
SECTION 4: Site of Care Restrictions
================================================================================

No site-of-care restriction applies for oral direct anticoagulants dispensed through outpatient channels.

================================================================================
SECTION 5: Dosing and Administration
================================================================================

Standard dosing is 5 mg twice daily with lower dosing permitted when patient-specific reduction criteria are met.
